# Rivyo FAQ

## General
Q: What is Rivyo?
A: All-in-one Shopify app — reviews, loyalty, referrals in one flat-fee plan. Built by WebContrive.

Q: Is Rivyo free?
A: Yes. Free plan (Indie) includes unlimited reviews, CSV import, auto requests, replies. Paid plans from $9/mo.

Q: What platforms?
A: Shopify only. Works in Shopify admin, supports latest themes.

Q: Who built it?
A: WebContrive.

Q: Where to install?
A: https://apps.shopify.com/rivyo-product-review

## Reviews
Q: What review types?
A: Written, photo, video (Enterprise). Store reviews and product reviews both supported.

Q: Can I import reviews?
A: Yes — CSV, Amazon, AliExpress, Judge.me, Loox, Stamped, Yotpo.

Q: Google Rich Snippets?
A: Yes, all plans including free.

Q: Auto review request emails?
A: Yes, from Starter plan. Set delay after fulfillment.

Q: Show reviews at checkout?
A: Yes, Shopify Plus stores only, Enterprise plan.

Q: AI review summary?
A: Yes, Enterprise plan only.

Q: What is product grouping?
A: Group multiple products so their reviews are combined and shared across all products in the group. Business plan+.

Q: What widgets are available?
A: Product review, rating badge, store reviews, average rating, popup, carousel, testimonials, Q&A, social sharing, all-reviews page.

## Loyalty
Q: What actions earn loyalty points?
A: Purchases, signups, writing reviews, engagement.

Q: What can points be redeemed for?
A: Discounts and perks.

## Referrals
Q: How does referral work?
A: Customer shares a unique link. Friend places an order → both earn rewards.

Q: Do referral points expire?
A: No expiry.

Q: Social sharing?
A: Yes, social and email sharing included.

## Pricing & Billing
Q: Revenue share?
A: No. Flat fee only on all plans.

Q: Annual discount?
A: Yes, ~20% off vs monthly.

Q: Change plans?
A: Yes, anytime via Shopify app subscription.
